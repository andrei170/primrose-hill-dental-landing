Your dentist

Dr Nyan Kyaw

Dr Kyaw qualified at Guy's & St Thomas' Hospital in London in 1999 and has cared for patients across London ever since. He became principal of Primrose Hill Dental Practice in 2015 and is known for being a calm, careful and genuinely kind dentist. He continues to train at the highest level, including postgraduate implant dentistry at the Royal College of Surgeons.

  • BDS, qualified at Guy's & St Thomas' Hospital, London (1999)
  • Principal dentist since 2015 (GDC no. 77160)
  • Special interests: implants, orthodontics and periodontology
  • Postgraduate implant training, Royal College of Surgeons, London
